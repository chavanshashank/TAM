Technoarena 2015

Aqua Robo


In the wide field of robotics one has to face different kind of surrounding so to deal with it this event gives chance to elaborate ideas in the different medium.

Rules

  1. All participants are required to report 30 minutes before the event starts, to the reporting desk.
  2. Every participant should have his college identity card or at least one proof of identity. All the teams are required to bring the registration receipt with them.
  3. Any misbehaviour of participant during event or rounds may lead to eliminate from the round he/she is currently in.
  4. All the team members must be present during the event at the specified time. If the team is absent, it will be disqualified.
  5. No extra time will be given to any team under any circumstances. Decision of the judges is final.
  6. Organizers are not responsible for any loss in property, injury and delays caused by participants during the event.
  7. If there is any damage to the tools provided or instruments then the team to which the instrument was given would be liable to pay for the damage.
  8. Other instructions will be provided on the day of event.

    9. Dimensions and Fabrications:
    1. The machine should fit in a box of dimension 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm (l*b*h) at every given point of the race.
    2. The boat has to be controlled by a wireless/wired remote control mechanism throughout the competition.
    3. Remote control systems from toys might be used. Remote control systems available in the market may also be used.
    4. Balsa wood or fiber glass or any other light in weight but strong material can be used for the construction.

    Requirements:
    1. The machine can be controlled wirelessly. Off board power supplies are allowed. Refer below for further details on battery and power.
    2. Remote control systems from toys might be used. Remote control systems available in the market may also be used.

    Motor specifications :
    1. The boat can have an external D.C supply or an on board power supply to provide power to any mechanism requiring electric power.
    2. Only dc motor should be used for the power supply. Battery and Power
    3. The machine can be powered electrically only. Use of an IC engine in any form is not allowed. On board Batteries must be sealed, immobilized-electrolyte types (such as gel cells, lithium, NiCad, NiMH, or dry cells).
    4. Use of damaged, non-leak proof batteries may lead to disqualification. Maximum D.C power permitted is 24 Volts.
    5. The machine you build would be first checked for size and safety.
    6. The machine should not cause electrical power breakdown.
    7. The robot must not contain any combustible, corrosive, or otherwise dangerous materials.
    8. Explosive, compressive or expansive, either internal or external is not permitted.
