Technoarena 2015

Auto-CAD


The engineer, especially a mechanical engineer needs a thorough knowledge of the working principles of any mechanism he deals with sound knowledge of engineering drawing “Modelling and Drafting” principles is even more essential for any engineer to take full advantage of the CAD system capabilities. This event will create interest and motivate him in this direction. Hence, students from mechanical engineering will keenly desirous to participate in this event.

Rules

  1. The participant should produce valid ID cards of their respective colleges.
  2. Calculator is NOT allowed.
  3. One candidate can participate only at once i.e. Reentry is Not available.


Structure

    1. There will be three rounds which are as follows:
    2. Round 1: (2-D)
      • Participant has to draw an Isometric view of given drawing.
      • No short cut commands will be there on screen of Auto-CAD.
      • Participant has to give commands manually.
    3. Round 2: (LIFE LINE Round) Participant has to draw an Isometric view of given drawing WITHOUT USING MOUSE. LIFE LINE: Participant can use mouse only twice.
    4. Round 3: (3-D) Participant has to draw 3-D view of given drawing using mouse and short cut commands within time limits.

    3. You may register for multiple events.
