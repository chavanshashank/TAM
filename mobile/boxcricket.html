Technoarena 2015

Box Cricket


Rules

  1. Both Boys and Girls teams are allowed.
  2. There will be separate matches held for Boys as well as Girls teams.
  3. The team would comprise of 8 players,only 1 substitution is allowed if necessary.
  4. The matches would be played with a soft tennis ball.
  5. Each innings would be of 3 over with each player bowling an over; no player can bowl more than one over.
  6. While bowling, the bowler cannot move or lift his leg, bowling will be done underarm.
  7. If the ball goes outside the playing area without bouncing even once in the playing area, the batsman would be given out.
  8. The batting team can score a maximum of 4 runs per ball because 6’s would be considered as out.
  9. The batting team can score a maximum of 4 runs per ball because 6’s would be considered as out.
  10. Students from different institute can form a team.
  11. One player can only be a part of one team however if any player found in more than one team, this will lead to disqualification of concerned teams.
  12. Organizers will have the right to modify any rules if needed to ensure the game is played in the right spirit.
  13. Decision made by the umpires will be upheld as the final decision, arguments will not be entertained.
  14. College ID for all participants is mandatory.
