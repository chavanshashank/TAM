Technoarena 2015

Crazy Designers


Crazy Designers is something to show about your talent in designing web applications. It is the way at which level you use html tags and create the attractive web pages. You can present your topic effectivelyand creatively.So, to show off your designing skills, this is the best platform. Do participate and have Fun.....!

Rules

  1. It is an individual and group event.
  2. Each person can register once.
  3. The event will have 3 rounds.
  4. Participants are not allowed to bring any media devices such as pen drives, CD,DVD, etc.
  5. Cell phones must be switched off.
  6. Any violation of rule may lead to disqualification.
  7. Apart from Rules, some additional rules will be disclosed on the spot.
  8. The decision of the organizers is final and binding.


Structure

    The crazy designers is the three round event. The individual or group (maximum two) can participate. The first round is multiple objective round in which questions are based on HTML. In second round, you have to create an attractive HTML Page with maximum tags. In third round, you have to create an HTML page as per the given format.

    1. There will be 3 rounds which is presenting the paper.
    2. Round 1: Multiple Choice Questions based on HTML.
    3. Round 2: You have to create an attractive HTML Page with maximum tags.
    4. Round 3: You have to create an HTML page in minimum time as shown to you.
