Technoarena 2015

Electroblitz


A 3-way challenge to test your technical and non- technical virtues via aptitude-test, conceptual knowledge base and ability to explore.

Rules

  1. It is an individual and group event.[Each participant can register only once]
  2. Each participant will be required to bring their college ID with them.
  3. Any violation of rules may lead to disqualification.
  4. Cell phones are not allowed during event.
  5. Apart from these rules, some additional rules will be disclosed on the spot.
  6. Decision of event organizers is final and binding.


Structure

    1. Electroblitz mainly consist of 3 rounds.
    2. Round 1: General Aptitude-test
      Top 20 participants will be selected for Round 2. 45 questions will be given to solve in 30 minutes.
    3. Round 2: Visual Quiz
      Questions will be based on basic technical knowledge. Visual Clue of the question will be displayed on the screen. Top 5 will be selected for Round 3
    4. Round 3: Circuit Hunt
      Participants will be required to build a simple circuit given on spot. Required materials will be provided on spot by the organizers. Top 2 groups completing the task in minimum time slot will be declared as winner of the event.


Register

    1. Entry Fees:
      • Individual: Rs.100/-
      • Per Group(Max 2 Members): Rs.200/-
    2. Complete your registration and confirm participation.
    3. You may register for multiple events.
      1. Please pay cash at the GCOEJ Info Desk in your college.
        2. OR

        3. OR
      3. On the Spot Registration if Entries are not full.
