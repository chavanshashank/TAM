Technoarena 2015

Explora


We need to understand that everything depends on an individual's talent & his skills. As marks are like water vapour which evaporates before talent & skills. For this purpose, Explora is conducted, which gives an idea about Student's attitude, aptitude & vision. It helps to enhance skills like logical thinking, grasping, general awareness of an individual. So, oppurtunity is at your door, just grab it & explore your 'Talent'.

Rules

  1. Students have to Participate individually and not in groups.
  2. Students should report 10 minutes before the event & should bring their receipt & I-card along with them.
  3. Judges decision will be final.
  4. Participants have to clear all four round, which will be conducted in the event for being a winner.


Structure

    1. Round 1: This round contains 30 aptitude questions for 30 minutes. The participants which are selected in this round will enter in next round.
    2. Round 2: Welcome to the round which provides you the freedom of discussing your ideas about the current events (and/or general topics) taking place around us in our day-to-day life. i.e group discussion
    3. Round 3: There will be surprise round conducted in this round to check inter-personal skills.
    4. Round 4: The participants which clears these all 3 rounds enter in the last round which is an Extempore.
