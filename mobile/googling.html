Technoarena 2015

Googling


Googling is considered as a type of web sport. The main objective is to find out the candidate having a vast technical knowledge & having command over the Internet and to find out the fastest user over the Internet. Candidates have to show their Internet browsing command to find out queries of technical concerns, Puzzles, General knowledge, Language etc.
