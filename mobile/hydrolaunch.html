Technoarena 2015

Hydro Launch


“We are using hydrolic energy along with pressure energy to launch a projectile”.

Rules

  1. The participants whose projectile will cover the maximum distance will be winner of hydro launch
  2. Judge decision will be final decision.


Structure

    1. There will be 3 rounds.
    2. Round 1:
      1. Inclination of model as well as level of water can be changed.
      2. There are only two chances for each pair.
    3. Round 2:
      1. Inclination of model remains same.
      2. Only water level in bottle can be changed.
    4. Round 3:
      1. Only inclination can be changed.
      2. water level remains fixed.
