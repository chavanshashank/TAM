Technoarena 2015

Lathe War


Lathe war is the competition, in which we check the practical knowledge of the students regarding the different machining process which is widely carried out in the industrial areas.

Rules

  1. Lathe operation should be perform as per supplied Job Drawing.
  2. Do not use any Polishing Material.
  3. Clean the machine after the completing the job.
  4. No further work piece and tool will be provided on Breaking. And he / she is supposed to be Disqualified.
  5. Safety precaution should be taken under performance on machine by every Participant.
  6. All issued equipment and tool to the participants should be submitted to the volunteer after the completion, if fails, liable against to action.
  7. Judicial Decision is a Final Decision.


Structure

    1. There Will Be three round which are as follows:
    2. Round 1: The first round will be multiple objective type in which full of technical based on completely manufacturing.
    3. Round 2: Second round will be the job making on the lathe machine as per the job drawing provided to the student.
    4. Round 3: Third round will be the also job making on the lathe machine as per the job drawing provided to the student.
