Technoarena 2015

Mud Robo


Design a machine (manual control) which is capable to run with opponent's in the arena.

Rules

  1. Only DC motors (Up to 24 volt).
  2. Batteries are allowed or Power supply will be provided.
  3. Time elimination.
  4. Three hand touch are allowed.
  5. Maximum 2 members in group.
  6. During the race operating wire should be slack.
  7. Juges decision will be final decision.

    8. Robot specifications:
    1. Your robot must fit into the cube of 30*30*30 cm, exceeding the limits will lead to disqualification.
    2. Maximum weight is 3 kg.

    Judging Criteria & Arena (Track):
    • In lowest time who cover the track with minimum hand touch will be winner.
