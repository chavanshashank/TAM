Technoarena 2015

Paper Presentation


Rules

    • Each team consists of 2 members only.
    • Only abstract should be send through e-mail.
    • Registration Fees Rs.50 (REGISTRATION) + Rs.250 (PARTICIPATION)
    • While mailing the abstract mention the Receipt number and branch as the subject.
    • Send abstract in PDF format only
    • The selected teams have to bring a soft copy of paper in CD/USB Device and 3 hard copies of paper at the time of presentation.
    • Total no. of words of the paper should not exceed 150 words.
    • Last date to send the soft copies is 26/02/2015.
    • ID card is must for the participants.
    • All communications will be only through email.
    • Presentation will be 7 min. which is followed by a question and answer section for 2 min.
    • The authors' email ID and contact no. should be mentioned.
    • Spot Entries are NOT accepted.
    • Directly copying from the Internet is strongly discouraged and will not be entertained.
    • Participation certificate will be given only to the participants.
    • Violation of any rule can result in rejection of paper.
    • Decision of the judges and the event heads shall be treated as final and binding on all and cannot be contested
    • All the rights are reserved with Techno Arena 2015 event committee.

      • Team Formation :
      1. No participants can be part of more than one team.
      2. It is not necessary that the participants forming a team should be from the same college.
      3. There is no restriction on the number of teams from the same college.

      Paper Format Rules :
        The paper should be in IEEE format.
      • Abstract may not exceed the limit of two Pages.
      • The front page should have title of paper on the top and the details of the team members at the bottom. (As mentioned in abstract).
      • It should have abstract attached.
      • Font style for text: Times New Roman.
      • Font size for text: 12 points.
      • Font size for headings: 16 points.
      • Font size for sub-headings: 14 points.
      • Margin of 1 inch from all sides.
