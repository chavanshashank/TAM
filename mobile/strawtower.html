Technoarena 2015

Straw Tower


By using drinking straw and tape to make tower and it must have maximum load with minimum deflection (deflection=25mm).

Rules

  1. Tower must be fit on its base.
  2. Tower must be of maximum height 45cm.
  3. Tower must have two stories with truss each of 18 cm in height.
  4. No restrictions on cross sectional area of tower.
  5. Tower must be able to carry of testing station.
  6. The weight applied will be constant.
  7. The tower which bears maximum load will be the winner.
  8. In case of tie between two teams the tower which will carry the maximum load with minimum deflection will be the winner.
  9. The appearance of tower will also considered.
      10. Material used for making tower.
    • 100 drinking straw.
    • 1 meter of 1inch masking tape.
    • paper clips [one box]

Structure

    There is only one round, 3 hours will provide to make a straw tower.
