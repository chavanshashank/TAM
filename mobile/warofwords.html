Technoarena 2015

War of Words


WORDS ==> Handle them Carefully, for words have more power than Atom Bombs... There is indeed power in words. Most of the lasting change that has been forged in the history of this world came not from a wielding of the swift and bloody sword of battle but from the shaping scalpel of ideas, and what are ideas without the words to deliver them? George Bernard Shaw had said that "If you have an apple and I have an apple and we exchange these apples then you and I will still each have one apple. But if you have an idea and I have an idea and we exchange these ideas, then each of us will have two ideas." This is the event we aim to provide a platform to bring forth the ideas, where people learn, empower themselves and evolve to be winds of change our country needs.

Rules

  1. Each participant will be required to bring their college ID with them.
  2. Any kind of misbehavior in the campus during the event will not be tolerated.
  3. Exceeding the provided time limit will result in penalties.
  4. The judge's word is the LAW.

Structure

    It is a 3-tier event. Each participant will be required to use their word power in order to advance in next round.

    1. Round 1- Elimination:
      Each participant will be given a set of question paper consisting of 20 questions each. Question will be based on general knowledge, current affairs and various quotes given by well-known persons.
    2. Round 2- On The Spot:
      The participants will be required to speak over a topic for two minutes, both 'for' and 'against' the topic. He/ She will have to switch over from 'for' to 'against' (or vice versa) after one minute.The switch over between the two parts must be smooth and fluid. Exceeding the time limits will result in penalties.
    3. Round 3- Debate:
      Participants advanced to this round will be segregated into two groups. One "in favor" and other "against" the motion.The topic will be published on the Techno Arena website one week prior the event and the participants will be required to visit the website to get hold of the topic. Details will be provided before the commencement of the round.The decisions of the Event Organizers are final and abiding to all participating teams.
